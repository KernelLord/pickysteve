# Create Pitch Deck

Use when building or tightening an investor pitch deck for an accelerator or a
pre-seed/seed raise.

## Capabilities
- The canonical 10-12 slide arc: problem, why-now, solution, product, market (TAM/SAM/SOM),
  business model, traction, competition, team, ask + use of funds.
- What each slide must *prove* (not just say) and the one number that anchors it.
- Narrative: the wedge, the inflection, and the "why this team, why now" thread.
- Tightening: cutting filler slides, turning features into outcomes, the appendix strategy.

## Notes
Deck structure and story. Pairs with cfo-advisor for the model behind the numbers and
competitive-landscape for the competition slide.

# Brand Voice

Use when content needs to sound consistently like a specific brand or person, derived
from real examples rather than invented.

## Capabilities
- Builds a source-derived style profile: sentence rhythm, vocabulary, formality,
  signature moves, things this voice never does.
- Applies the profile to new copy — landing pages, posts, emails, docs.
- Flags off-voice drafts and rewrites them in-voice.
- Keeps a do/don't list grounded in the actual samples, not generic "be friendly" advice.

## Notes
Voice/style, distinct from campaign strategy (marketing-campaign).

# Competitive Landscape

Use when you need to understand who you are up against and where the open space is —
for a strategy call, a competition slide, or a positioning decision. Typical triggers:
who else is building this, size up the other players in our space, where can we win.

## Capabilities
- Enumerates direct, indirect, and "status quo / do nothing" competitors.
- Builds a positioning map on the two axes that actually matter to buyers.
- Finds white space and the wedge — where you win before you expand.
- Honest teardown of competitor strengths, not just their gaps.
- Frames differentiation as outcomes for a specific ICP, not feature checklists.

# Marketing Campaign Planner

Use when planning or executing a product launch or marketing campaign and you need a
plan tied to a business outcome. Typical triggers: improve my marketing, plan a
go-to-market push, drive more signups, get the word out about our launch.

## Capabilities
- Sizes the funnel backward from the goal (e.g. signups) to required top-of-funnel.
- Picks a channel mix for the actual audience and budget, not by reflex.
- Messaging: the core promise, proof, and CTA per channel and funnel stage.
- A measurable plan with the one metric per stage that says it is working.
- Identifies the broken funnel stage when an existing campaign underperforms.

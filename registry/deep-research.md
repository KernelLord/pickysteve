# Deep Research

Use when a question needs current external information from multiple sources, not
just reasoning over what is already in context. Typical triggers: dig up everything
published on a topic, pull together what the sources say, find all the recent work on X.

## Capabilities
- Plans a research question into sub-queries and searches multiple angles.
- Reads and cross-checks sources; separates primary sources from secondary commentary.
- Synthesizes into a structured answer with inline citations and a confidence note.
- Flags disagreement between sources rather than averaging it away.
- Distinguishes "what the sources say" from "what is true."

## Notes
External knowledge gathering. Not for reasoning over the local codebase.
